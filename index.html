Here's a little about me!

My favourite type of music is Rock. I especially like the band Arctic Monkeys. I like the genre because Rock music is unique, seen in how upbeat and energetic it is. The voices of the people who sing rock music are also very vocally gifted, since they must have a powerful voice.


The lead singer in Arctic Monkeys is Alex Turner. He is 38 years old and was born on January 6, 1986. He is known for his lyrics, which consist of clever wordplay, and oftentimes deep meaning.
